Weekly Skin‑Support Meal & Activity Tracker

Low‑activity friendly, high protein, skin‑nourishing foods — View Skincare Plan

Plan Notes & Guidelines
▶

Goals

Calories: ~1,650–1,800/day

Protein: 110–140 g/day — this means foods like chicken, lean beef, eggs, Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, or **plain protein powder** (whey, casein, or a plant blend). Use “unflavored” or “vanilla” powder with no added sugar, and measure with the scoop it comes with — usually 20–25 g protein per scoop.

Water: 2.5–3 L/day

Skin Support

**Vitamin C foods**: berries, kiwi, bell peppers — help your body make collagen.

**Healthy fats**: nuts, seeds, olive oil — keep skin’s outer layer strong.

**Zinc/Copper**: meats, nuts, seeds — support elasticity & repair.

Activity

Each “Move” in the table says exactly how to do it and how long. They’re short, gentle, and can be done from a chair unless noted.

1–2 min break each hour works better than doing them all at once.
