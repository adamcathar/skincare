Weekly Meal Plan Tracker

Low-activity, high-protein, low-sodium. No turkey. No seafood.

Plan notes & guidelines
▶

Targets

Calories: ~1,600–1,800/day

Protein: 110–140 g/day

Water: 2.5–3 L/day

Season with herbs, spices, lemon/vinegar. Skip added salt to keep sodium in check.

Meal building

Protein first: Eat protein before carbs to steady blood sugar.

Slow-burn carbs: Sweet potato, quinoa, brown rice, couscous (measured cooked).

Veg volume: 1–2 cups non-starchy veg at lunch/dinner.

Swap options

Chicken ↔ pork tenderloin ↔ lean beef (90%+). Dairy and eggs for snacks.

Plant protein: beans/lentils/cottage cheese/Greek yogurt as needed.

This plan excludes turkey and seafood by design.
