Plan Notes & Guidelines
▶

Goals

Calories: ~1,650–1,800/day

Protein: 110–140 g/day — e.g., chicken, lean beef, eggs, Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, or plain protein powder (20–25 g protein per scoop, no sugar)

Water: 2.5–3 L/day

Skin Support

Vitamin C foods (berries, kiwi, peppers) for collagen synthesis.

Healthy fats (nuts, seeds, olive oil) for skin barrier health.

Zinc/copper (meat, nuts, seeds) for elasticity.

Activity

Moves are short & gentle; can be done seated. The “Move” column tells you exactly what to do and for how long.

Better to sprinkle through the day than cram all at once.
