Goals

Calories: ~1,650–1,800/day

Skin Support

Vitamin C foods (berries, kiwi, peppers) for collagen synthesis.

Healthy fats (avocado, nuts) for skin barrier health.

Zinc/copper (meat, nuts, seeds) aid elasticity.

Activity

Daily moves boost circulation & deliver nutrients to skin.

1–2 min movement breaks each hour.
