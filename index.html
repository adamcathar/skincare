Monthly Skincare Routine

Manly, clean, and low‑shine — mapped for the whole month

Product Information

Toner — Witch Hazel + Tea Tree + Willow Bark

Balances skin, controls oil, preps for serums. Mist 3–4 sprays from 6–8 inches away; air‑dry 30–60s.

Vitamin B, C, E & Niacinamide Serum

Brightens, strengthens, controls oil. Use 3–4 drops AM, pat in, avoid eyelids.

Hyaluronic Acid (HA) Serum

Pure hydration without heaviness. 3–4 drops, pat in.

HA + Rice Ferment Night Booster

Hydrates and brightens. PM only, 3–4 drops.

Niacinamide 5% + Squalane Serum

Oil control + light hydration. PM on assigned nights, 3 drops.

Collagen Liquid Serum

Supports firmness. PM on assigned nights, 3–4 drops.

Retinol Serum

Cell turnover, anti‑ageing. PM only, twice per week, 2–3 drops. Avoid eyes. Wait 2 min before next step. No masks/Vitamin C same night.

Collagen Cream

Light moisture & firmness. Half‑pea AM daily; PM on retinol nights. Apply perimeter of face.

Vitamin C Cream

Brightens, evens tone. PM only, not with retinol.

Night Mask — Peptides + Ceramides

Overnight hydration, barrier support. Thin layer on assigned nights; avoid T‑zone if oily.

Lavender Melatonin Eye Cream

Soothes under‑eyes. Rice‑grain per eye nightly; dab gently.

Masks

  • Hydrating Clay: 10–15 min
  • Peel‑Off Charcoal: 20 min
  • Kaolin/Bentonite/Charcoal: 10 min max
  • Gold Peel‑Off: 20 min
