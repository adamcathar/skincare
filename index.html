Product types, functions, and application tips Guide
Product Type Main Function Key Ingredients Application Tips
Cleanser Removes dirt, oil, buildup without stripping Glycerin, Ceramides, Aloe, Low‑pH surfactants AM & PM; massage 30–60 sec; lukewarm rinse
Hydrating Toner / Essence Boosts hydration, preps for serums Hyaluronic Acid, Panthenol, Green Tea, Rice Extract Apply with hands or cotton; pat until absorbed
Vitamin C Serum Brightens, supports collagen, antioxidant shield L‑Ascorbic Acid, Ferulic Acid, Vitamin E AM only; 3–5 drops; wait 1–2 min before layering
Niacinamide Serum Minimizes pores, balances oil, evens tone Niacinamide 5–10%, Zinc PCA, Allantoin AM or PM; layer after Vitamin C or toner
Hyaluronic Acid Serum Hydrates, plumps by attracting moisture Multi‑weight HA, Vitamin B5, Glycerin Apply to damp skin; pat into cheeks/forehead/jawline
Retinol Serum Cell turnover, anti‑wrinkle, clears pores Retinol 0.3–0.5%, Retinal, Bakuchiol PM only; T‑zone & cheeks; avoid immediate heavy occlusion
Niacinamide + Squalane Serum Barrier support, calming, oil regulation Niacinamide, Squalane, Ceramides Recovery nights; full face after toner
Collagen / Peptide Serum Firmness, elasticity, anti‑aging support Peptides (Matrixyl/Argireline), Collagen, Amino Acids Cheeks, jawline, neck; PM after actives
Lightweight Moisturizer Seals hydration, supports barrier Ceramides, Glycerin, Squalane, Panthenol AM; even layer to face & neck
Barrier Cream / Night Mask Deep overnight hydration and repair Ceramide NP, Shea Butter, Peptides, Allantoin PM; perimeter on retinol nights; full face on recovery nights
Eye Cream Puffiness, fine lines, dark circles Caffeine, Peptides, HA, Vitamin K AM & PM; gentle dab under eyes and crow’s feet
Clay Mask Detox pores, absorb excess oil Kaolin, Bentonite, Charcoal, Sulfur 1–2×/week; T‑zone only; rinse 10–15 min
Chemical Exfoliant Removes dead skin, smooths texture, clears pores AHA (Glycolic/Lactic), BHA (Salicylic), PHA PM 1×/week; T‑zone + cheek center; avoid eye area
Sheet Mask Hydration and soothing infusion HA, Collagen, Green Tea, Ceramides 1–2×/week; after toner; 15–20 min
Sunscreen (SPF 50+) UV protection to prevent photoaging Zinc Oxide, Titanium Dioxide, modern chemical filters AM; face/ears/neck; reapply every 2–3 hours outdoors
