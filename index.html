7‑Day Skincare Routine

Detailed, step‑by‑step grooming plan for healthy, clean, and low‑shine skin

How to Cleanse

  • Wet face with lukewarm water.
  • Use a nickel‑sized amount of gentle cleanser.
  • Massage in small circles for 30–45 seconds.
  • Rinse thoroughly, pat dry — no rubbing.

Product Guide

Toner — Witch Hazel + Tea Tree + Willow Bark

Balances skin, controls oil, preps for serums. Mist 3–4 sprays from 6–8 inches away; air‑dry 30–60s.

Vitamin B, C, E & Niacinamide Serum

Brightens, strengthens, controls oil. Use 3–4 drops AM, pat in, avoid eyelids.

Hyaluronic Acid (HA) Serum

Pure hydration without heaviness. 3–4 drops, pat in.

HA + Rice Ferment Night Booster

Hydrates and brightens. PM only, 3–4 drops.

Niacinamide 5% + Squalane Serum

Oil control + light hydration. PM on assigned nights, 3 drops.

Collagen Liquid Serum

Supports firmness. PM on assigned nights, 3–4 drops.

Retinol Serum

Cell turnover, anti‑ageing. PM only, twice per week, 2–3 drops. Avoid eyes. Wait 2 min before next step. No masks/Vitamin C same night.

Collagen Cream

Light moisture & firmness. Half‑pea AM daily; PM on retinol nights. Apply perimeter of face.

Vitamin C Cream

Brightens, evens tone. PM only, not with retinol.

Night Mask — Peptides + Ceramides

Overnight hydration, barrier support. Thin layer on assigned nights; avoid T‑zone if oily.

Lavender Melatonin Eye Cream

Soothes under‑eyes. Rice‑grain per eye nightly; dab gently.

Masks

  • Hydrating Clay: 10–15 min
  • Peel‑Off Charcoal: 20 min
  • Kaolin/Bentonite/Charcoal: 10 min max
  • Gold Peel‑Off: 20 min

7‑Day Schedule

Day 1

AM: Cleanse → Toner → Vitamin B/C/E + Niacinamide → HA Serum → Collagen Cream → Sunscreen

PM: Cleanse → Hydrating Clay Mask → HA + Rice Booster → Niacinamide + Squalane → Eye Cream → Night Mask (skip T‑zone)

Day 2

Same as Day 1 AM

PM: Cleanse → Retinol Serum → HA Serum → Collagen Cream (perimeter only) → Eye Cream

Day 3

Same as Day 1 AM

PM: Cleanse → Peel‑Off Charcoal Mask → HA + Rice Booster → Collagen Serum → Eye Cream → Night Mask (cheeks only)

Day 4

Same as Day 1 AM

PM: Cleanse → Niacinamide + Squalane → Collagen Serum → Vitamin C Cream (cheeks only) → Eye Cream

Day 5

Same as Day 1 AM

PM: Cleanse → Kaolin/Bentonite/Charcoal Mask → HA + Rice Booster → Collagen Serum → Eye Cream → Night Mask (perimeter only)

Day 6

Same as Day 1 AM

PM: Cleanse → Retinol Serum → HA Serum → Collagen Cream (perimeter only) → Eye Cream

Day 7

Same as Day 1 AM

PM: Cleanse → Gold Peel‑Off Mask → HA + Rice Booster → Collagen Serum → Eye Cream → Night Mask (cheeks only)

Routine designed for a clean, healthy, low‑shine look — consistent use = visible results.
